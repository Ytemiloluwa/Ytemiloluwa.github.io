Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How does EmotiQ analyze my emotions through voice?
A: EmotiQ uses Digital signal processing and Speech recognition to analyze vocal patterns, tone, and speech characteristics that correlate with emotional states. Our Dual pipeline technology processes these vocal cues to provide insights into your current emotional state and patterns over time.
Q: Is my voice data secure and private?
A: Absolutely. Your voice data is processed securely with our third-party service provider ElevenLabs. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete details.
Q: Do I need an internet connection to use EmotiQ?
A: Yes, EmotiQ requires an internet connection for voice analysis and AI coaching features.

Subscription & Billing

Q: What's included in the free version?
A: The free version includes basic voice analysis with limited daily check-ins. Premium features include unlimited voice analysis, personalized AI coaching, voice affirmations, and detailed emotional analytics.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > EmotiQ > Cancel Subscription. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refund requests are handled through Apple's App Store policies. You can request a refund directly through Apple within the eligible timeframe after purchase.

Technical Support

Q: EmotiQ isn't recognizing my voice properly. What should I do?
A: Ensure you're in a quiet environment and speaking clearly into your device's microphone. Check that EmotiQ has microphone permissions enabled in your iPhone's Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > EmotiQ.
Q: Failed to record Audio for emotion Analysis.
A: After voice cloning set up you might encounter "failed to record audio error". Restart the app for an audio clean up.
Q: The app is crashing or not loading properly.
A: Try these troubleshooting steps:
  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Ensure you have the latest version of EmotiQ from the App Store
  4. Check that you have a stable internet connection
Q: My emotional analytics aren't showing up.
A: Analytics require multiple voice check-ins over time to generate meaningful patterns.

Features & Usage

Q: How often should I use EmotiQ?
A: We recommend daily check-ins for the best insights into your emotional patterns. However, you can use EmotiQ whenever you want to understand your current emotional state or need coaching support.
Q: Can I use EmotiQ if English isn't my first language?
A: EmotiQ is optimized for English speakers. While it may work with accented English, accuracy may vary. We're working on expanding language support in future updates.
Q: How do voice affirmations work?
A: Premium users can create personalized affirmations that are generated in a voice similar to their own. This feature uses advanced voice synthesis technology to create meaningful, personalized content.
